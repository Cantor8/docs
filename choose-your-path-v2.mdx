## What are you trying to do?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I'm building an application and need users to connect a wallet" defaultOpen icon="plug">
    You want **Wallet SDK**.

    A drop-in integration layer that lets your dApp connect users to C8 Wallet, request approvals, and submit Canton transactions — without managing keys, auth, or ledger plumbing.

    <Card title="Go to Wallet SDK →" icon="arrow-right" href="/wallet-sdk/introduction">
    </Card>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I'm an institution and need to hold and move Canton assets at scale" icon="building-columns">
    You want **Enterprise Wallet**.

    Multi-party custody and operations for banks, funds, custodians, and treasury teams. Role-based controls, approval policies, multi-asset balances, and audit-ready logging.

    <Card title="Go to Enterprise Wallet →" icon="arrow-right" href="/enterprise-wallet/common-use-cases">
    </Card>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I'm launching a token, stablecoin, or tokenized asset on Canton" icon="coins">
    You want **Token Factory**.

    A ready-made issuance layer for stablecoins, securities, RWAs, and loyalty assets. Mint, burn, transfer, and registry controls without writing DAML or maintaining infrastructure.

    <Card title="Go to Token Factory →" icon="arrow-right" href="/token-factory/common-use-cases">
    </Card>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I'm doing more than one of the above" icon="layer-group">
    Many teams use multiple products together. Common combinations:

    - **Issuer:** Token Factory (issue) \+ Enterprise Wallet (treasury) \+ Wallet SDK (end-user access)
    - **dApp \+ institutional users:** Wallet SDK (UX) \+ Enterprise Wallet (custody for institutional accounts)
    - **Asset manager:** Enterprise Wallet (custody) \+ Token Factory (issue fund units)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
