One table, three products. Find the row that matches your team.

|  | <Icon icon="plug" />**Wallet SDK** | <Icon icon="building-columns" />**Enterprise Wallet** | <Icon icon="coins" />**Token Factory** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Who it's for** | dApp developers | Institutions and operations teams | Issuers of tokens, stablecoins, RWAs |
| **Main job** | Connect users to C8 Wallet | Hold and move Canton assets at scale | Create and manage Canton-native assets |
| **Use when…** | You need wallet auth, approvals, and transfers in your dApp | You need multi-party custody, policies, and audit | You're launching a new on-ledger asset |
| **Skip when…** | You're not building a user-facing app | Single-user or end-user scenarios | You only need to hold or transfer existing assets |
| **Typical user** | DeFi, payments, trading, treasury dashboards | Banks, funds, custodians, brokers | Stablecoin issuers, asset managers, loyalty programs |
| **Owns keys?** | No — C8 Wallet does | Yes — with policy-based access | N/A — issuance only |
| **Integration style** | Drop-in JS SDK | Operated wallet service | Issuance \+ registry service |
