Interested in integrating Cantor8 Token Factory into your product or operations?

The C8 team will guide you through the integration end-to-end — scoping the right token setup, provisioning access, and walking through technical onboarding.

  Reach out to [**hello@cantor8.io**](mailto:hello@cantor8.io) and we'll get back to you with next steps.

## What to include in your message

To speed things up, share a short note covering:

- Your company and the product you're building
- What you want to tokenize (asset type, jurisdiction, scale)
- Expected go-live timeline
- Any specific compliance or operational requirements

Once we have that, the C8 team will schedule a kickoff call and share the technical onboarding materials.
